About My Content


As my content revolves around cybersecurity, my goal is to make people aware about the security risks of technology, and give advice to protect themselves.

By showing how different types of malware attacks, my goal is to show the viewer how easy it is to be affected by this, and how to tell if a PC is infected.


As there are many different types of malware, I aim to show many, from different time periods to exactly show how each infect and affect a system.

Along with coverage of malware, I may sometimes do tutorials on how to do certain things inside an OS, or using a certain program, such as VMWare.




Quick Security Tips


Keep your Anti-Virus & Anti-Malware protection up to date.

Avoid clicking on suspicious links & advertisements.

Stay anonymous online & don't give out any personal information.
